About

We are Uncharted Limbo - a collective of creative coders and visual artists, crafting groundbreaking New Media Art at the unlikely intersections of code, interactive visuals, and performing arts.

Fusing our individual expertise in the fields of Computational Design, Game Development, VFX, Film & Architecture, we founded the Collective to work in ways far greater than the sum of our parts.
