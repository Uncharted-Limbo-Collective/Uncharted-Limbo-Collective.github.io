About

We are Uncharted Limbo - a collective of creative coders & visual artists based in London.

We are a network of close friends, with a shared curiosity for what lies beyond current technological boundaries and at the unlikely intersections of code, design, sound and visual arts.

Fusing our individual expertise in the fields of Computational Design, Game Development, VFX, Film & Architecture, we founded the Collective to work in ways far greater than the sum of our parts.
