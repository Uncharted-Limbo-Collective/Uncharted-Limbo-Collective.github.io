Chaotic Body II - Liminal Phase

September 2021

Chaotic Body is a triptych of films, developed in collaboration with choreographer Alexander Whitley. The project investigates the very nature of Dance in a socially distanced world. This second film named "Liminal Phase" features a dance duet and emphasizes on the chain of actions/reactions the dancers' bodies trigger in a vast field of digital matter.

Description

This film focuses on the hidden materiality of movement and how it can manifest itself as digital matter, affected by the various forces generated from the dancers' bodies.

This manifestation of invisible energy comes to life both emphatically, through sharp and swift movements, but also subtly, by paying attention to the human breath and how it can dissolve the boundaries between the form of the dancers' bodies and the environment in which they are situated.

In the vat of digital matter, the dancers are moving through computationally recreated fluids with various properties that celebrate the flow of energy around us.
