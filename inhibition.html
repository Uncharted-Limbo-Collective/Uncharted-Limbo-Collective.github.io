Inhibition

October 2021

Inhibition is an immersive experience that challenges you to rethink your subjective reality as you journey with two other audience members on a quest around an imaginary island.

­
­
­

Description

Collaborating with UK-based artists Zoe Diakaki and Marina Eleni Mersiadou who were inspired by A B Casares’ novel The Invention of Morel, we developed an immersive multi-user experience that was featured in BFI London Film Festival 2021 as part of its Expanded section.

This project incorporates biofeedback, skeleton tracking and the recreation of the landscape and atmosphere of the deserted island in Casares' novel. An immersive and performative experience unfolds in three scenes, following the trajectory of the novel’s protagonist.
